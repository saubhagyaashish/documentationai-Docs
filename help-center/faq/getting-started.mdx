## Quick Start

To begin with Saubhagya Ashish:

1. Sign in at `https://saubhagyaashish.com`
2. Click "Create Project"
3. Enter your project details
4. Save and start documenting

## Where are my docs?

All your documentation is organized under "My Projects" in your dashboard. Select a project to view or edit docs.
